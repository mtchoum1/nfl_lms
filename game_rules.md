# Last Man Standing Rules
1. Each week each pick team to win and you can't pick that team every again
2. You can't pick a team from the same division until all other division are used once reseting every 8 weeks (optional)
    - You can't pick a team from the same division until all other division are used once on 8 week rolling bases (optional)
3. If your team loses you lose you are elimated (optional)
    - If you team loses you lose team points (optional)
4. If your team wins you get points based on team points Equation
5. If your team tie you get half of total points ((team 1 points) + (team 2 points)) / 2
6. If you get back to back correct teams after elimation you get back into the pool ( All other rules still apply and Tie counts as a win in this sceranio) (optional) ( number of games can be set)
7. When you are elimated if you win you get 0.5 * (team points equation)

### Team Points Equation
points for team with (+) odds are calculated with this equation: (1 - (100/(odd + 100))) * 100
points for team with (-) odds are claculated with this equation: (1 - (abs(odd)/(abs(odd) + 100))) * 100
